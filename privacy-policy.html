Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect your data.

Last Updated: March 6, 2025

Introduction

ToolkitProo ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and calculators at toolkitproo.online.

Information We Don't Collect

We designed ToolkitProo with privacy in mind. Our calculators operate entirely in your browser, which means:

  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not store your calculation inputs or results
  • We do not use cookies for tracking
  • We do not require user accounts or registration
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties

Information We May Collect

The only information we may have access to includes:

  • Server Logs: Basic server logs that may include your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This is standard web server functionality used for security and troubleshooting.
  • Contact Form: If you contact us through our contact form, we receive the information you voluntarily provide (name, email, message).

How We Use Information

Any information we collect is used solely for:

  • Responding to your inquiries
  • Improving our website and calculators
  • Maintaining website security
  • Analyzing aggregated usage patterns (without personal identification)

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Website hosting
  • Analytics (anonymized)

These services may have access to basic server logs but do not receive personal information from us.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect any information in our care. However, since we don't collect personal calculation data, your financial inputs remain entirely on your device.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
