Calculator-Specific Questions

PMI (Private Mortgage Insurance) is typically required when your down payment is less than 20%. You can manually estimate PMI at 0.5-1% of your loan amount annually and add it to your calculations.
Our VAT calculator supports the three main UK VAT rates: 20% standard rate, 5% reduced rate, and 0% zero rate. Simply select the appropriate rate for your calculation.
